Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

WatchVault is a movie and TV show tracking app. All your data — your "already seen" list, watchlist, swipe history, ratings, and custom lists — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Data Storage

All app data is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate any servers or databases. Your data is not backed up to any cloud service operated by us. Standard iCloud device backups may include app data per your Apple settings.

Third-Party Services

This app uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to retrieve movie and TV show information including titles, descriptions, posters, and ratings. When the app fetches this information, standard network requests are made to TMDB's servers, which may log technical data such as your IP address in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We encourage you to review TMDB's privacy policy at: themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

We do not send any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers to TMDB or any other third party.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies of any kind.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with an updated date.
